Room Details

These huge rooms are sumptuously styled with art, design classics and everything from a 23 carat gold leafed headboard in one, to a freestanding silver roll top bath in another. All have a large lounge area, fireplace and a separate large bathroom with bath, monsoon shower and toilet.

  • Breakfast & afternoon tea and cake included
  • King-sized beds with hand-stitched Egyptian cotton linen and bathrobes
  • Hand-crafted decor such as tadelakt or carved plasterwork
  • Artwork from Vanessas private collection
